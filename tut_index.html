💡 Universal Breach Prevention Tips (All Programs)
🔹 Daily Loss Limit: Stop trading immediately when you hit 50% of daily loss. Don't chase.
🔹 Max Drawdown: Treat it as a hard floor. Never risk more than 1-2% per trade.
🔹 Consistency Rule: If firm has a 30-40% best day rule, spread profits across multiple days.
🔹 News Trading: Unless explicitly allowed, avoid trading 5 minutes before/after high-impact news.
⚠️ FXIFY Instant Funding - Drawdown Lock Explained (Most Misunderstood Rule)
How it works: Your Max Trailing Drawdown starts at -8% from starting balance (e.g., $10,000 account → $9,200 floor).
As you profit, the floor trails upward. The lock triggers when: (a) you reach 8% profit from start, OR (b) you request your first payout — whichever comes first.
After lock, the drawdown becomes static at your original starting balance ($10,000 floor).

🚨 DEADLY TRAP: If you withdraw 100% of profits after lock, your balance resets to $10,000. The next trade's spread (1-2 pips) drops equity below $10,000 → HARD BREACH.
✅ SOLUTION: Always keep at least 1-2% of profits in the account as a buffer. Withdraw 80-90% of profits only.
